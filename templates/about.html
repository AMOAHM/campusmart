About Campus Mart

Our Mission

Campus Mart is dedicated to empowering student entrepreneurs and connecting them with campus customers. We believe in creating a thriving marketplace where everyone can buy and sell with confidence.

Why Choose CampusMart?

  • Easy to Use: Simple interface for both buyers and sellers
  • Safe Transactions: Payment on Delivery for peace of mind
  • Live Support: Chat with support and store owners in real-time
  • Wide Selection: From fashion to food, books to services
  • Community Focused: Supporting campus enterprises

How It Works

For Customers
  1. Browse products and services
  2. Add items to cart
  3. Checkout with delivery info
  4. Receive order at your hostel
  5. Pay on delivery
For Entrepreneurs
  1. Register with admin approval
  2. Get your store setup
  3. Add products/services
  4. Manage customer orders
  5. Receive payment from customers
